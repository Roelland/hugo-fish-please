Every visit is tailored to your tank's specific needs. Here's what's included:

### {{< icon name="droplet" >}} Regular Feeding

I follow your exact feeding schedule and portions and can handle flakes, pellets, frozen food, or live food.

### {{< icon name="eye" >}} Monitoring

I'll observe your fish for signs of stress, disease, or injury and contact you immediately if anything looks off.

### {{< icon name="camera" >}} Photo Updates

You'll receive a photos and/or videos of your tank after every visit, so you can relax knowing everything is fine at home.


---
And also provided extra services, like:

### {{< icon name="plant-wilt" >}} Plant Care

For example - trimming fast-growing plants, topping off evaporation water.

### {{< icon name="temperature-half" >}} Water & Equipment Checks

Checking water temperature, filter function, heater and lighting schedules.

### {{< icon name="flask" >}} Water Testing

For stays of 5 days or longer I can test pH, ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate levels and take corrective action if needed.
