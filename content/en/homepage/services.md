Every visit is tailored to your tank's specific needs. Here's what's included:

### {{< icon name="droplet" >}} Daily Feeding

We follow your exact feeding schedule and portions — no overfeeding, no missed meals. We handle flakes, pellets, frozen food, or live food.

### {{< icon name="temperature-half" >}} Water & Equipment Checks

Each visit includes a check of water temperature, filter function, heater, protein skimmer (for saltwater setups), and lighting schedules.

### {{< icon name="flask" >}} Water Testing

For stays of 5 days or longer we test pH, ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate levels and take corrective action if needed.

### {{< icon name="eye" >}} Health Monitoring

We observe your fish for signs of stress, disease, or injury and contact you immediately if anything looks off.

### {{< icon name="camera" >}} Photo Updates

You'll receive a photo or short video of your tank after every visit, so you can relax knowing everything is fine at home.

### {{< icon name="plant-wilt" >}} Plant & Coral Care

Trimming fast-growing plants, spot-feeding corals, and topping off evaporation water — we've got the details covered.
