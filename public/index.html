Going away? Your fish stay happy.

FishPlease is a dedicated fish sitting service run by an aquarium enthusiast who understands that fish are real pets that deserve real care. Whether you have a freshwater community tank, a saltwater reef, or a single cherished betta, I treat every aquarium with the attention it deserves.

“Fish are friends!”

What We Do

Every visit is tailored to your tank’s specific needs. Here’s what’s included:

Daily Feeding

We follow your exact feeding schedule and portions — no overfeeding, no missed meals. We handle flakes, pellets, frozen food, or live food.

Health Monitoring

We observe your fish for signs of stress, disease, or injury and contact you immediately if anything looks off.

Photo Updates

You’ll receive a photo or short video of your tank after every visit, so you can relax knowing everything is fine at home.

And also provided extra services, like:

Plant & Coral Care

Trimming fast-growing plants, spot-feeding corals, and topping off evaporation water — we’ve got the details covered.

Water & Equipment Checks

Each visit includes a check of water temperature, filter function, heater, protein skimmer (for saltwater setups), and lighting schedules.

Water Testing

For stays of 5 days or longer we test pH, ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate levels and take corrective action if needed.

Not sure which plan fits your tank? Get in touch and we’ll figure it out together.

About Me

Fish, Please was started by an aquarium hobbyists that wanted to go on a holiday but didn’t find anybody to watch their fish. By the lack of good fish care options whenever they travelled. Dog and cat sitters are everywhere — but who looks after your reef tank?

We are based in The Hague and also server places like Voorburg en Rijswijk.

Our values

  • Fish first — your tank’s wellbeing always comes before convenience.
  • Transparency — you get a photo update after every single visit.
  • No surprises — fixed prices, clear communication, no hidden fees.
  • Passion — we genuinely love aquariums. This is not just a job.
